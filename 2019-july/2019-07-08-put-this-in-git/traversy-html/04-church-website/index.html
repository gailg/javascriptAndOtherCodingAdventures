Christian formation

The Montessori-inspired Godly Play approach helps children explore their faith through story and ritual, teaches religious language to allow them to express their growing faith, and enhances their spiritual experience through wonder and play

Life in our community

Join caring ministries such as Eucharistic Visitors, Stephen Ministers, Helping Hands, and the Prayer Shawl Ministry. The Hospitality Committee organizes coffee hours, receptions, special events, and newcomer gatherings.

Our church in the world

We have many service opportunities including Davis Community Meals, St Michael's food and toiletry baskets, Interfaith Rotating Winter Shelter, and Wednesday night dinners at the Belfry. The Care for God's Creation Committee advocates environmental stewardship including becoming a zero carbon church.
